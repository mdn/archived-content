An array of property IDs.

Description

JSIdArray is used to hold IDs for enumerated properties associated with an object.

Use {{jsapixref("JS_IdArrayLength")}} and {{jsapixref("JS_IdArrayGet")}} to access the property, and {{jsapixref("JS_DestroyIdArray")}} to free, or use {{jsapixref("JS::AutoIdArray")}} as a local variable.
