This page lists the errors that can be raised due to Firefox's anti-tracking functionality, governed by the Storage access policy. You can find further information about them by clicking on the links below:

A request to access cookies or storage was

  • blocked because of a custom cookie permission
  • blocked because it came from a tracker and content blocking is enabled
  • blocked because we are blocking all storage access requests
  • blocked because we are blocking all third-party storage access requests and content blocking is enabled
  • granted partitioned access because it came from a third-party and dynamic state partitioning is enabled
