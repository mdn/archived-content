void didMerge(

  in nsresult aMergeResult
);
Parameters
aManager
The nsITransactionManager ending a batch.
aTopTransaction

aTransactionToMerge
The transaction to merge.
aDidMerge

aMergeResult
The nsresult returned after the merge attempt.
